Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Do I need a membership or a business account?

No.

How do I place an order?

First check the restaurant you want to order from and Waiters on Wheels hours to see if both are open for business. Then, give us a call and an operator will assist you in placing your order. For faster service please have the name of restaurant and item numbers ready to order. You can access menus from our home page or at the bottom of any page.

How long does it take for an order to be delivered?

Usually an order takes about 45 minutes. Please ask your WOW operator for a specific time. Orders can be placed ahead of time and or for a future date and time.

How much does it cost?

$4.95 flat rate for all delivery orders.

Should I tip?

Absolutely. Our drivers go to the restuarant, put in your order, and bring you your food. The cost of the delivery is not a tip- this is a service charge to keep us running. If you are happy with the service, you should definitely tip your driver.

Who determines the menus and sets the menu prices?

Restaurants determine menus and menu prices which are subject to change without notice.

What payment methods can I use?

We accept cash and all major credit cards.

Is there a minimum on orders?

Yes. There is a $15 minimum on all orders.

If I have a question about my order who do I contact?

For any questions concerning an order please call us at (508) 754-3663 or email wowaccount@gmail.com
